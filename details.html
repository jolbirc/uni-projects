About The Property

Our modern space-themed guest suite is located in the heart of historical Chester, designed with those looking for a space getaway in mind! As well as the high-tech simulation and gaming facilities on-site, the guesthouse is located in the city centre of Chester, close to local amenities and attractions, to provide all visitors with a complete getaway for all ages and occassions.


Our guesthouse is also located within a larger complex with an on-site bar, casino, a day-spa, and a small theatre that hosts two shows a day, four nights a week. Complete with food outlets, all your needs are catered for, and includes our rooftop taqueria!
